# Thư Ngỏ Mời Hợp Tác Nền Tảng Du Lịch Cộng Đồng LocalLife.Asia

LocalLife.Asia

Dear Valued Partner,

**Local Life Technology Co., Ltd (LocalLife.Asia)** is a pioneering digital platform in the field of **Community-Based Tourism (CBT)**, developed with the mission of curating and providing high-quality, authentic, and deeply human-centered local content.

Beyond applying technology to digitize local experiences, **LocalLife.Asia** aims to elevate Vietnam’s and Asia’s tourism landscape, promote cultural values to international audiences, and empower local communities — so that anyone can take ownership, tell their own stories, and generate income from what is most familiar to them.

We are especially seeking partnerships with **OTA** platforms like yours — organizations looking for unique solutions to deepen product offerings and build stronger connections with travelers who love immersive, culture-based, and nature-centered experiences.

What sets **LocalLife.Asia** apart is how we build our content: not from mass-sourced data, but through direct collaboration with local communities — including small homestays, independent guides, local artisans, cultural groups, and traditional community-based tourism villages. This approach ensures that every experience is authentic, context-rich, and reflective of the true spirit of each destination.

We will launch our first **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** in August with the participation of a select group of partners. Our data is standardized and available via **API** or popular formats (**CSV**, **JSON**), accompanied by technical support, documentation, and free trial access to facilitate your evaluation.

Our product ecosystem is clearly categorized for easy integration and marketing:

## I. Stay: Accommodation options that highlight local identity and community immersion.

### CBT Stay:
- Traditional Local Accommodation: Homestays, local homes, stilt houses, heritage houses.
- Nature & Eco Stay: Farmstays, glamping, camping, eco-lodges, nature resorts, small boutique hotels.

## II. Experience: Activities and tours designed to deeply engage travelers with local culture, nature, and community life.

### CBT Cultural & Nature Experiences:
Craft villages, festivals, farming, trekking, cloud hunting, yoga & meditation, ethnic cooking classes, picnics, family meals, traditional drink mixing workshops, cultural performances.

### CBT Thematic Journeys:
Half-day to multi-day tours, wellness & detox retreats, family packages, local team building, honeymoon & couple retreats, pre-wedding photo tours, volunteer & conservation trips, senior packages.

## III. Service: Add-on services to support and enrich the overall travel experience, often provided by individuals or local businesses.

### CBT Support & Utility Services:
- Local Shop: Online marketplace for local products.
- Personal & Convenience Services: Spa, massage, laundry, make-up, food delivery, medical support, professional photography, traditional costume rental.
- Transport & Guiding: Local guides, vehicle rental.
- Local Travel Support: Food & shopping recommendations, hidden spots.

We believe that **LocalLife.Asia**’s localized content will help your company:
- Deepen product authenticity and appeal to customers seeking real, immersive experiences.
- Increase conversion rates through emotional, context-rich, and user-reviewed suggestions.
- Strengthen brand identity as an **OTA** that connects deeply with communities and cultural values.
